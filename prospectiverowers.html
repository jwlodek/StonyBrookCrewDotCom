Coxswains are the leaders within the boat. Our boats consist of 4 rowers and 1 coxswain. The coxswain is in charge of the whole boat. They decide which way to steer and command the rowers. This position requires strong leadership skills and the ability to give directions to large groups of people. Coxswains are typically smaller in stature in order to fit in the coxswain's seat within the boat.
